CODIS Cold Case

Description

For this individual project, I researched how the Combined DNA Index System (CODIS) was used to solve a cold case—the 1996 Shenandoah National Park double murder. I explored how DNA evidence collected at the crime scene was preserved, retested decades later, and uploaded into CODIS to identify the suspect. The project highlighted the power of DNA technology in solving cold cases and emphasized the importance of expunging DNA from individuals not convicted of a crime to protect innocent people from wrongful inclusion in databases.
