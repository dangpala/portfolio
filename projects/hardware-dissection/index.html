Hardware Dissection Lab

Description

In this project, our group dissected a desktop computer to explore its internal components and understand how they function together. We identified key parts such as the CPU, RAM, GPU, SSD, power supply, motherboard, and input/output interfaces, noting details like the placement of the SSD above the RAM and the single 8GB RAM stick. This hands-on activity gave us a deeper understanding of how the hardware is organized and how each component interacts to make a computer function.
